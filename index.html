Demo Page for Univocal

Abstract: This page presents the audio samples for our submitted paper: UniVocal: Unified Speech-Singing Code-Switching Synthesis. It demonstrates the performance of our proposed method across three different tasks: Speech-Singing Code Switching (SCS) Synthesis, Textual Empathy TTS, and Singing Generation.
Note: All samples are generated unseen during training.

In this task, UniVocal generates code-switching audio solely from text input. For visualization purposes, black text denotes speech parts, while red text indicates singing parts.
